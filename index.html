Working with me means bringing someone onto your team who is self-directed, adapts quickly, and approaches development with a security-first mindset.

LearnEra — Founding Vision

Built in Bangladesh. For Bangladesh.

How It Started

LearnEra didn't start as a product — it started as a question: why does quality tech education cost so much in Bangladesh? In 2021, a small group of friends began meeting in an online study group, sharing resources and discussing ideas about how education actually works. That conversation grew into a commitment to build something real.

Today, LearnEra is a fully built, free-access online learning platform — designed from the ground up to give every Bangladeshi student a genuine shot at in-demand, job-ready skills, regardless of their financial situation.

"We'd rather prove our value first. Every course is free until December 31, 2027 — no hidden fees, no payment required."

The Mission

Make practical, project-based learning accessible to every Bangladeshi student — building real skills that employers and clients actually want, without a price tag as a barrier.

LearnEra Labs

A long-term R&D roadmap exploring localized AI tooling and shared learning infrastructure for Bangladeshi developers — open, community-driven, and built to last beyond the free period.

Verifiable Outcomes

Every completed course earns a free, verifiable certificate — permanently issued to the learner even after the free access window ends in 2027.
